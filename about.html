WHO I AM

"Clarity of purpose and simplicity are the essence of design. I design to accentuate the natural qualities of a space; amplifying its particular beauty through seamless functionality and unique form."

Vanshika

Vanshika is a leading designer of ultra-luxury estate homes in India.

Her versatile projects span a comprehensive range of luxury lifestyle creations. Infusing her experimental processes with scientific clarity and pragmatism, Vanshika creates exemplary spaces that range from awe strikingly expansive to stylishly intimate.

She is the founding principal and design director of a design and build firm that works across architecture, interiors, furniture, lighting, product design, and landscaping. She has handled a variety of projects including residences, commercial developments, retail outlets, restaurants, spas and pubs.

Vision & Mission

Our Mission

To redefine luxury living by designing spaces that seamlessly blend timeless elegance with cutting-edge functionality. We are dedicated to translating our clients' dreams into breathtaking realities, focusing on precision, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to unparalleled quality in every detail.

Our Vision

To be the benchmark for architectural excellence and ultra-luxury residential design in India and internationally. We envision a future where our creations stand as monumental testaments to artistry and sophisticated living, inspiring awe and setting new standards in the world of high-end design.
